Distribution Board

Rewiring of distribution boards & providing a protective fuse or circuit breaker for each circuit in a common enclosure to make them compliant. New installations are also done for new projects for commercial property and factories.

Generator installations

Installations and supply of backup power solutions are provided by Nasco to ensure even in the darkest of times your business and homes are connected and still operating efficiently. We are not limited to generators we also install inverters that will also keep you powered up through loadshedding schedules.

Electrical Rewiring

Nasco assists in getting rid of the old wires and replacing them with new higher load ones. It's most likely that there might be other upgrades too, like replacements of sockets and adding brand-new powerpoints we do it all to ensure your home is safely connected and complaint.

Factory and home installations

Nasco covers all aspects of electrical, we do maintenance and installations of electrical machinery in factories and homes. Nasco also does repairing & modernizing of machinery in factories and homes.

LIGHTING INSTALLATIONS & REPAIRS

At Nasco we ensure quality lighting products are provided with a great warranty. We ensure lights are power saving efficient and give out the best qaulity light for your needs, whether home,factory or growing lights we have approved suppliers for every lighting need.

Certificate of Complaince

Nasco ensures your electrics are up to standard with regularatory laws and extensively checks your electrics are done safely and issues out the COC. We also recommend areas where improvement on your electrics is required.
